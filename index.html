About Zaydi Forces

We are a symbol of resilience and truth. In a world that tries to erase our story, we write it in bold. This platform is our voice — and our weapon.

This movement unites all Shia branches under one banner of resistance. Our name does not symbolise Zaydi doctrine of Shi'ism but Ithna Ashari.
